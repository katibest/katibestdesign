EngageWIT

2020

Overview

EngageWIT is a platform designed to connect women in technology with meaningful mentorship opportunities. The project aimed to create a seamless experience for both mentors and mentees, facilitating meaningful connections and professional growth in the tech industry.

My Role

As the lead UX/UI designer, I was responsible for creating the user experience and interface design for the platform. This included conducting user research, creating wireframes, designing the user interface, and collaborating with developers to ensure successful implementation.

Process

The design process began with extensive user research and interviews with potential users. We identified key pain points in existing mentorship platforms and developed solutions to address them. The design evolved through multiple iterations, incorporating user feedback at each stage. The final design focused on creating an intuitive matching system and fostering meaningful connections between mentors and mentees.
