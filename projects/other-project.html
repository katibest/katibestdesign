Practical Product Designer Series

Overview

In this ongoing series, I'm diving into the world of new and upcoming technologies and tools to explore how far I can go as a product designer with front-end coding skills and a bit of backend knowledge. Using Cursor as a key tool, I'm challenging myself to see what I can create on my own, pushing the limits of what a designer can accomplish without a developer's direct assistance.

Through the series, I'll be documenting my journey, starting with story mapping and wireframing, and then bringing the project full circle by building out a functional prototype. Each episode will cover the tools, techniques, and challenges I encounter along the way. Currently, there are two episodes out, with new ones releasing weekly as I continue to explore, experiment, and learn.
