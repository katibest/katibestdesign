Patient Reach 360

As the UX and UI Designer for Patient Reach 360, I focused on creating an intuitive and efficient platform for healthcare providers. The challenge was to design a system that could handle complex patient data while remaining accessible and easy to use for medical professionals with varying levels of technical expertise.

I conducted extensive user research with healthcare providers to understand their workflow and pain points. This research informed every aspect of the design, from the information architecture to the visual interface. The result was a platform that not only met the technical requirements but also enhanced the user experience for healthcare professionals.

Project Overview

Patient Reach 360 needed a comprehensive solution to manage patient outreach and engagement. The existing system was fragmented and inefficient, leading to missed appointments and poor patient communication. Our goal was to create a unified platform that would streamline these processes and improve patient outcomes.

The new platform needed to handle appointment scheduling, patient reminders, and communication tracking while maintaining HIPAA compliance. We focused on creating a system that was both powerful and user-friendly, ensuring that healthcare providers could easily manage their patient relationships without getting lost in complex interfaces.
