Family Nurturing Center

2023/2024

Overview

The Family Nurturing Center project involved redesigning their digital presence to better serve families in need. The goal was to create a more accessible and user-friendly platform that would help connect families with vital resources and support services.

My Role

As the UX designer, I led the research and design process, working closely with stakeholders to understand the needs of both the organization and the families they serve. I created user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes to guide the development process.

Process

The project began with stakeholder interviews and user research to understand the needs of families seeking support. We conducted usability testing with both staff and families to identify pain points in the existing system. The design process focused on creating clear pathways to resources while maintaining privacy and dignity for users.

Project Details

  • Client Family Nurturing Center
  • Timeline 2023-2024
  • Services UX Research, UX Design, UI Design

The new design has significantly improved our ability to connect with families. The intuitive interface makes it easier for families to find the resources they need, and the streamlined process has reduced the time it takes to get help to those who need it most.
