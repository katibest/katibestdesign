A Little More About Me

When I'm not crafting digital experiences, you'll find me exploring life's little joys and adventures.

Through My Lens

Photography has been my creative outlet for years. There's something magical about capturing a moment in time, whether it's the golden hour light filtering through leaves or the candid expressions of my four-legged coworkers.

You can follow our adventures on Instagram at @ohrocketdogs where I share snapshots of our daily life and the occasional behind-the-scenes of my design work.

Green Thumb Adventures

My garden is my sanctuary. There's something therapeutic about getting your hands dirty and watching things grow. From succulents to seasonal flowers, each plant has its own story and personality.

It's not always perfect (those pesky squirrels!), but that's part of the charm. Gardening has taught me patience and the beauty of imperfection – lessons that often find their way into my design work.

Bookworm Life

I'm a self-proclaimed bookworm, always with a stack of books waiting to be read. From design theory to fantasy novels, each book offers a new perspective and inspiration.

My husband and I often have reading dates – there's nothing better than cozying up with a good book and a cup of tea. He's my biggest supporter and the one who keeps me grounded when I get lost in my creative world.

My Support System

Behind every creative person is a support system that keeps them going. My husband is my rock – he's the one who brings me coffee during late-night design sessions and reminds me to take breaks when I'm too focused.

And of course, there are our four-legged family members who make every day an adventure. They're not just pets; they're my daily dose of joy and inspiration. Each one has their own personality that adds color to our lives.
